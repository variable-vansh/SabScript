# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Offer actionable feedback.
- Welcome newcomers and different perspectives.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

For now, report violations through a private maintainer channel of your choice.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially as much as possible.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
